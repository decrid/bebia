# Bebia pay model plan

Date: 2026-04-14

## Recommended model

Use a freemium subscription, not an upfront paid app.

Why:
- Parents need to trust the app before paying.
- Bebia gets stronger when users build a daily habit first.
- The strongest paid value is recurring guidance, not one-time setup.

## What should stay free

- Core logging for feeding, sleep, diaper, and crying
- Timeline with edits and deletes
- Child profiles
- Basic family preparation
- Lightweight AI helper that proves the product works

## What should become premium later

- Weekly AI briefing
  Plain-language weekly summary of patterns, changes, and pressure points.
- Predictive routines
  Wake windows, likely next event windows, and proactive routine coaching.
- Crying intelligence history
  Cause patterns, confidence trends, and what helped in similar moments.
- Reports and export
  Doctor-friendly summaries, caregiver handoff reports, and shareable exports.
- Advanced family coordination
  Smart handoff notes, shared reminders, and role-specific caregiver views.

## Bebia Plus package

Name:
- Bebia Plus

Positioning:
- Do not sell logging.
- Sell calmer decisions, faster understanding, and less mental load.

Three value pillars:
- Interpretation
  Weekly AI briefing and crying intelligence history.
- Prediction
  Stronger predictive routines and proactive suggestions.
- Coordination
  Reports, exports, and advanced multi-caregiver workflows.

## Suggested launch order

Phase 1:
- Weekly AI briefing
- Predictive routines

Phase 2:
- Crying intelligence history
- Reports and export

Phase 3:
- Advanced family coordination

## Best upgrade moments

- After 7-10 days of data, show the first weekly AI briefing teaser.
- After repeated prediction usage, offer predictive routines as a time-saving upgrade.
- After multiple crying analyses, offer crying intelligence history.
- Before doctor visits or caregiver handoffs, offer reports/export.
- When multiple caregivers actively use the app, offer advanced coordination.

## Packaging recommendation

- Free: daily use, trust, habit, and enough AI to show value
- Plus monthly: deeper AI, patterns, prediction, and exports
- Plus annual: same benefits with discount, pushed only after clear retention

## Rollout plan

1. Keep the app fully useful without payment.
2. Improve retention and perceived quality first.
3. Build 2-3 premium features with obvious weekly value.
4. Add soft upgrade surfaces after an AI success moment.
5. Test monthly and annual subscription only after users repeatedly use the assistant layer.

## Product principle

Do not charge for recording care.
Charge for understanding, prediction, and time-saving coordination.
